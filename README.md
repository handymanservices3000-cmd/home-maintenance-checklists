# Home Maintenance Checklists

Free home maintenance checklists, seasonal maintenance guides, DIY repair tips, and homeowner resources.

## Why Home Maintenance Matters

Regular home maintenance helps prevent costly repairs, improves safety, increases property value, and extends the life of your home's major systems.

---

# Spring Home Maintenance Checklist

- Inspect roof for winter damage
- Clean gutters and downspouts
- Check siding and exterior paint
- Wash windows
- Service lawn equipment
- Inspect fences and gates
- Pressure wash decks and patios
- Test smoke and carbon monoxide detectors
- Replace HVAC filters
- Check outdoor faucets for leaks

---

# Summer Home Maintenance Checklist

- Inspect deck boards and railings
- Seal wood surfaces if needed
- Trim trees away from the roof
- Clean dryer vents
- Check irrigation system
- Inspect caulking around windows and doors
- Touch up exterior paint
- Inspect garage door operation

---

# Fall Home Maintenance Checklist

- Clean gutters
- Winterize outdoor faucets
- Service furnace
- Replace HVAC filters
- Inspect attic insulation
- Seal air leaks
- Clean fireplace and chimney
- Inspect weather stripping
- Drain garden hoses

---

# Winter Home Maintenance Checklist

- Monitor ice dams
- Test GFCI outlets
- Check plumbing for freezing risks
- Inspect crawl spaces
- Keep walkways clear
- Test emergency lighting
- Inspect garage door seals

---

# Monthly Home Maintenance

- Test smoke detectors
- Check plumbing for leaks
- Replace HVAC filters when needed
- Clean kitchen exhaust filters
- Inspect caulking
- Tighten loose hardware
- Check doors and windows
- Inspect flooring
- Look for drywall cracks
