Why This Knowledge Center Exists

Owning a home is one of life's biggest investments. Proper maintenance helps reduce expensive repairs, extends the life of building systems, improves energy efficiency, increases property value, and creates a safer living environment. The Home Maintenance Knowledge Center was created to provide homeowners with practical educational information that is easy to understand and continually updated with new resources.
